Visual Design

UX, UI, Identity

Identity Design: I take care of all visual aspects that make up your brand including colour, typography, patterns, imagery and logos delivered in a complete Brand Style Guide.

Web Design: I start with user experience design determining user personas and user flow then building sitemaps and wireframes before moving on to interface design, incorporating your brand's look and feel.

Web Development

HTML, CSS

Hand-Coded HTML: I write the code based on the final web design with a focus on clean, well formatted, semantic HTML5. This ensures your web content is accessible and simple to expand upon.

Well-Organized & Responsive CSS: Working as your designer & developer means I can ensure the final website is a pixel perfect match to the design and do so with CSS that is not only mobile responsive but also easy to read and build on.

A little bit about me...

Textiles, Interiors

My career started in textile sales & interior design where I was so inspired by the colour combinations and sophisticated patterns in high end fabrics. I loved building colour palettes, mixing materials and sketching floor plans.

Data Analysis, Visualizations

My logical and practical self pulled me in a technical direction to the communications industry where I worked in data and business analytics however I still felt an urge for a creative outlet. I discovered how to present my work in meaningful ways by telling a story with data visualizations.

Web Design, Development

Then I discovered web design & coding. In the past, I always felt like I was either pushed too far into a creative field or pulled too far into a technical direction, but the combination of visual design and web development seems to be the perfect balance of art and science.

Let's work together

Now, I feel so lucky to be able to do what I do. I'm passionate about creating beautiful, online spaces and always try to create something unique and personal for my clients. I'd love to do the same for you!
